Hockey & Sporting Goods

Canadian Global Logistics is your trusted partner in the transportation of hockey and sporting goods. With us, you can be assured of a smooth, hassle-free logistics experience that lets you focus on the game, not the gear.

  • Experience You Can Trust: Leverage our more than 25 years of expertise in handling all aspects of importing and exporting sporting equipment with the utmost precision and care.
  • Efficient Air Service: Our timely air service options ensure your shipments arrive when and where they need to. Whether single or consolidated shipments, we manage everything from cost-efficient transportation to smooth customs clearance.
  • Ocean Freight Options: Benefit from our economical ocean freight services, ideal for non-urgent, off-season orders. We even offer direct shipping from Asia to European buyers for maximum efficiency.
  • Seamless Bond Shipments: Experience the ease of our bond shipping process. We can collect and ship US orders with other cargo, ensuring all your goods remain in bond and avoid unnecessary border clearance procedures.
  • Comprehensive Warehousing: Enjoy the convenience of no-charge warehousing while your orders are being collected for export, providing flexibility and security.
