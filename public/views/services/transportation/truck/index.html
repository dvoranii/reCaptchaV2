Our bonded delivery service ensures the secure and efficient transportation of your goods. Bonded goods are those imported into a country for which duties and taxes are deferred until they are taken out of bond, which is particularly advantageous for clients dealing with high-value merchandise. This enables you to keep your merchandise safe and secure, while also offering significant cost savings.

In addition, we understand the pressing need for timely deliveries in today's fast-paced business environment. That's why we offer tailgate and time-sensitive appointment deliveries. With our tailgate delivery services, we bring your freight directly to your doorstep, ensuring it arrives securely and on time. Our time-sensitive appointment deliveries are crafted to meet the specific needs of our clients who require precise scheduling. With this service, you can rest assured that your delivery will reach its destination exactly when it needs to be there.


Moreover, Canadian Global Logistics provides the added benefit of utilizing our own truck fleet for local pickups and deliveries. This means we maintain complete control over the delivery process, ensuring a high level of reliability and efficiency. Our dedicated team of drivers and logistics experts work together to ensure your freight is handled with the utmost care and professionalism.


For clients dealing with US IN BOND cargo, we offer a seamless solution of picking up your cargo and keeping it in our secure, bonded facility. This allows you to consolidate your orders, matching up different shipments and sending them all at once. This method not only streamlines your logistics but also defers the payment of customs clearance, duties, and taxes in Canada until the goods are taken out of bond. This strategic approach to handling your logistics can result in significant financial savings.
